Where your donation goes

A simple loop. No bureaucracy.

1

You give.

Whether $25 or $2,500, every donation goes into the next grant cycle. We're an all-volunteer board.

2

Teachers apply.

DPS educators submit proposals three times a year. Our board reviews every application on its merits.

3

Students benefit.

Programs run within the school year. We share photos and stories with the community when they do.

Our story

A foundation built by Dedham, for Dedham.

In 1995, a small group of Dedham parents and teachers noticed something. Some of the most memorable moments in school — a working scientist visiting a 4th grade classroom, a touring theater company performing Shakespeare for middle schoolers — were exactly the kinds of things public school budgets struggled to support.

They started DEF as a way to fill that gap. Thirty years and over a million dollars in grants later, we're still doing it the same way: locally, transparently, with an all-volunteer board.
